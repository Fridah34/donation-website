ABOUT

At Donatia, our mission is to create a meaningful and lasting impact in the lives of vulnerable individuals and communities across Kenya. We believe that everyone deserves access to basic necessities, quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for a better future. Through the power of collective giving, we connect kind-hearted donors with those in need, ensuring that every contribution—big or small—makes a tangible difference.


Our goal is to provide sustainable solutions that address poverty, hunger, education gaps, and healthcare challenges, empowering individuals to build self-sufficient and thriving communities. We are committed to transparency, accountability, and compassionate service, working closely with local partners and organizations to maximize the effectiveness of our efforts. By fostering a culture of generosity and social responsibility, we aim to inspire change and bring hope to those who need it most. Together, we can transform lives and build a brighter future for Kenya. Join us in making a difference—because every act of kindness counts!
